\section{Introduction}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \emph{\textbf{Project Overview:} Briefly describe the algorithmic trading strategy project, including the objective to enhance the baseline strategy using S\&P 500 historical market data and earnings call transcripts.}

This project focuses on developing and enhancing an algorithmic trading strategy by leveraging S\&P 500 historical market data and earnings call transcripts. The core objective is to build upon a baseline trading strategy, integrating quantitative technical indicators with qualitative insights derived from natural language processing (NLP) of corporate earnings communications. By analyzing price movements, trading volumes, and sentiment from transcripts, the project aims to create a more robust and predictive trading model that can generate profitable signals in the dynamic stock market environment. 
    \item \emph{\textbf{Motivation and Background:} Explain the significance of algorithmic trading strategies in modern finance and how big data analytics and natural language processing can provide valuable insights.}

Algorithmic trading strategies have revolutionized modern finance by enabling high-speed, data-driven decision-making that minimizes human bias and emotional influences. In an era where markets operate 24/7 and process vast amounts of data in real-time, these strategies provide a competitive edge through automation and precision. Big data analytics plays a crucial role by processing large-scale historical datasets to identify patterns and trends that traditional methods might overlook. Complementing this,  NLP extracts valuable insights from unstructured text data, such as earnings call transcripts, which often contain forward-looking statements, risk disclosures, and market sentiment indicators. Together, these technologies allow traders to incorporate both quantitative metrics (e.g., price volatility, volume spikes) and qualitative factors (e.g., management confidence, economic outlook) into their strategies, leading to more informed and adaptive trading decisions. 
    \item \emph{\textbf{Objectives:} Clearly state the goals of the project, including specific improvements targeted over the baseline strategy.}

The primary goals of this project are to systematically improve upon a baseline algorithmic trading strategy through iterative development and evaluation. 

Specific objectives include: 
    \item (1) implementing and optimizing technical indicators such as moving averages, RSI, MACD, and Bollinger Bands for signal generation; 
    \item (2) applying NLP techniques, including FinBERT-based sentiment analysis, to earnings transcripts for enhanced predictive power; 
    \item (3) exploring scalable computing methods to handle large datasets efficiently, reducing computational latency; 
    \item (4) conducting rigorous performance evaluation using proper train/validation splits to ensure model generalization; and 
    \item (5) achieving measurable improvements in key metrics such as Sharpe ratio, maximum drawdown, and cumulative returns compared to the baseline approach. 

Through this process, the project seeks to demonstrate the practical value of integrating big data analytics and NLP in algorithmic trading. 
\end{itemize}
